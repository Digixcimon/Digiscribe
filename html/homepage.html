About Us

A Philippine-based Medical Transcription Company Serving the Information Technology Enabled Services (ITES) Sector.

Our Story



Founded in 2003, the company pioneered medical transcription services operating at Batangas Province. The company has a global client base from the United States, Australia, United Kingdom, Canada and Singapore.

It is well-regarded for delivering high-quality transcription services at very affordable rates.

Over the years, it has built a strong reputation for accuracy, reliability, and fast turnaround times. The company employs a team of highly skilled transcriptionists, editors, and quality assurance specialists who ensure that every document meets international standards.

Its commitment to continuous improvement and customer satisfaction has earned the trust of healthcare professionals and institutions worldwide. Leveraging the latest technologies in secure data transmission and voice recognition, the company remains at the forefront of the medical transcription industry.
